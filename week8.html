Lecture

In class we learnt about typography and how it will set the overall tone of our websites, what I found the most interesting were the examples that were shown on the lecture near the end, while each website was easily to read they all conveyed a different mood by using a combination of colour, font choice, size and weight. This had me thinking of my font choices, the message that I want to convey was simple elegance. My rough idea for fonts were clean lines but also something soft and feminine. After looking at font combinations I settled on Playfair Display and Montserrat because I thought that they met the requirements of the website
