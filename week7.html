Assignment Progress

During the break I sketched out all the wireframes needed, I used Behance to get an idea of how people would typically layout their wireframes. I iterated the wireframes and recreated them on Figma. The one think that I wasn’t sure about was the blog page because it is supposed to have a chill curated look to it but it did not work with any grid style that I created. I talked to Shiv about it and he said that I didn’t have to follow the grid but I would have to justify why I did that in documentation, so what I learnt was that it is good to follow the grid when creating wireframes but there are times where you can stray from the template if there is a certain look that you are trying to achieve. I also did some research on colours and I decided to go with a neutral colour palette to give a clean, simple luxury aesthetic.
